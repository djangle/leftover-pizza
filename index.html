Tripping the fantastic mundane.

Right now, in our galaxy and others not so different... You occupy something sci-fi, quarters of a space station? ship? Your home is a single 3D wall, how high does it go? Large windows of the galaxy, obscured by space junk; books, goggles, a cornucopia of interstellar goods. You see a ladder, impassable at the moment, a screen with a flashing green light, and what looks like your bunk covered in blankets and bedsheets (clothes?) Moving to one side of the room presents you with the option of moving to the Lintel. The Lintel move matter between eachother at great distances. You don't know anything else, and you only use it as directed by your screen. Do you have room/shipmates? You find you are the current occupant-- junk from other "guards" (custodians?) litter your bedroom. The screen directs you the Lintel, and the Lintel brings you anywhere, anytime, in any of the verses throughout the multiverse. The road... transhallucinagenic jazz. Anything can happen. Anything can be. Where to start?
